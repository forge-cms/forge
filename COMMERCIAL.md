# Commercial use

Forge is MIT licensed and always will be free for open source projects,
personal use, and commercial use of any kind.

## Future plans

When a hosted offering ("Forge Cloud") becomes available, we plan to
introduce a commercial license tier for organisations running Forge
as a managed service for their customers.

This will never affect the open source core. The MIT-licensed framework
you use today will remain MIT forever.

## Contributor License Agreement

Because of this plan, contributors are asked to sign a simple CLA
before their first pull request is merged. This grants forge-cms the
right to include your contributions in commercial offerings.

The CLA is handled automatically via cla-assistant.io when you open a PR.

## Questions

If you have questions about commercial use, licensing, or the CLA,
open an issue tagged `licensing`.
